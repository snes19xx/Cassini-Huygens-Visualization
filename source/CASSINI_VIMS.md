The Visual and Infrared Mapping Spectrometer (VIMS) operates over a total wavelength range of 0.35 to 5.1 microns. The specific spectral limits for its two channels are:

- **Visible channel (VIMS-V):** Covers the wavelength region from **0.35 to 1.05 microns**.
- **Infrared channel (VIMS-IR):** Covers the wavelength region from **0.85 to 5.1 microns**.
